Remodelling your condo & hotel doors with Architectural Film?

There is no need to replace old doors when you can upgrade them at a fraction of the cost and time!

Architectural Film is well-suited for high traffic areas as they are abrasion resistant, making them ideal for buildings in need of updating without inconveniencing guests during renovation. Our finishes also provide hundreds of cost-efficient ways to update your space — with minimal downtime and noise. With multiple architectural vinyl finishes available you have the possibility to pick the one that best represents your building.

Comparing Door Remodelling Solutions

Typical building door renovations: applying veneer to your existing door, painting, replacing or resurfacing your hotel door with interior architectural door film. Both veneer and New doors allow you creative freedom in terms of design but don’t forget about its potential flaws – extremely expensive, hard to repair and its slow turnaround time. Painting your hotel doors increases the cost of maintenance, as they deteriorate quickly and are not abrasion resistant. Painting also limits the amount of design flexibility.
Our approach is also completely clean, quiet and odourless! Meaning there is no downtime for your business or interruptions to your guests. Architectural vinyl films are also available in many different colours, wood finishes, metallics and solids.
